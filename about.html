About the Library Management System

The Library Management System (LMS) is designed to streamline the day-to-day operations of a library. It helps librarians, students, and faculty manage books, track borrow/return activities, and recommend new titles.

With LMS, users can easily explore the library's collection, request books, and stay updated on due dates and availability. The system supports efficient cataloging and ensures a smooth workflow in educational institutions and public libraries.

This platform is user-friendly, responsive, and built using modern web standards to ensure accessibility and scalability.
